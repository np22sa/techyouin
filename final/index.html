WHAT WE DO?

You're going to fall in love with VR

My first VR Project intends to introduce kids the next generation of computing: Virtual Reality (VR). Children can now start being part of this technological revolution via developing their own Do-It-Yourself cardboard VR glasses and virtual contents for truly immersive experiences.

VR headset... Make it your own

Personalize the headset using colored pencils, markers and paintings.

Learn more →

VR Headset - Create and Personalize

To build the VR Headset we use the Google CardBoard template (V 1.2) which can be printed in two A4 sheets of paper. Afterwards, corrugated cardboard of 1.7mm of thickness can be used to assemble the structure of the headset which uses two biconvex lenses to generate the VR view by placing a smartphone inside.

Storyboard - Imagine a Story

Kids are encouraged to create their own stories using paper and pencils through a simplistic storytelling methodology. The idea is to imagine stories via defining characters, scenarios, dialogues, conflicts and conclusions of a complete novel story. A template to create a simple storyboard can be found here.

Create the VR world (with Cospaces)

The story created is represented in a virtual 3D world using a free online platform called CoSpaces (https://cospaces.io/edu/). There can easily be created whatever kids want in 3D and explore it in virtual reality through the android application. Thus, closing a creative VR loop allowing an immersion into the virtual environment just created.

Share with your friends and family

Share your work and knowledge with family and colleagues by downloading the papercardboard template and recreating the VR Headset. Show them how to use the software and keep exploring.
